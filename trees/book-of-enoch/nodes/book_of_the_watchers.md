# Book of the Watchers

## Summary
The Book of the Watchers tells of heavenly beings who descend to earth, take human wives, produce giant offspring, and teach forbidden knowledge, leading to divine judgment.

## Notes
Use this node to track Watchers traditions, Genesis 6 expansion, forbidden knowledge, giants, judgment, and later demonological interpretation.
