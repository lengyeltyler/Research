# Nephilim / Giants

## Summary
The Nephilim or giants are associated with the offspring of the Watchers and human women in Enochic interpretation. Their violence and corruption become part of the reason for divine judgment.

## Notes
Compare biblical Nephilim references with Enochic giants traditions.

## Sources
- Sefaria — Genesis 6
- Early Jewish Writings — 1 Enoch

## Claims
