# Heavenly Journeys

## Summary
Enochic writings often portray Enoch being taken through heavenly or cosmic realms, where hidden structures of creation, judgment, angels, and divine order are revealed.

## Notes
Use this branch to track visionary geography, cosmic order, judgment scenes, and heavenly intermediaries.
