# Watchers

## Summary
The Watchers are angelic beings in Enochic tradition who cross a boundary between heaven and earth and become associated with forbidden knowledge, corruption, judgment, and the origin of giants.

## Notes
Track names, roles, sins, taught knowledge, punishment, and later influence.
