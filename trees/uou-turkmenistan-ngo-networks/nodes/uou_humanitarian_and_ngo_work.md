# UOU humanitarian and NGO work

## Summary
The UOU reportedly performed practical civic work: NGO registration help, refugee support, enterprise registration help, and local training courses.

## Notes

The exact enterprises are unidentified. The massage courses are best read as ordinary vocational or therapeutic instruction; no evidence suggests sexual or illicit meaning.
