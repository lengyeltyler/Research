# Ovezberdy A. Muradov

## Summary
Ovezberdy A. Muradov was associated with Charjou/Turkmenabat ufology and appears as UOU president in 2004.

## Notes
Muradov is described in the UFOs: Fantastic Reality listing as a physicist and chairman of the Charjou City Association of Ufologists. Later records identify Ovezberdy A. Muradov as president of the UOU. The record supports organizational association, not ethnic inference or extraterrestrial conclusions.
