# Humanoid Encounter Archives

## Summary
A research cluster for humanoid encounter catalogues as claim archives, folklore databases, mythology-evolution material, and anthropological datasets.

## Notes
The archive is not treated as proof of aliens. It preserves reported claims, recurring motifs, chronology, and cultural transformations: folklore beings, religious visions, contactee entities, robotic figures, abduction structures, and post-Soviet paranormal narratives.
