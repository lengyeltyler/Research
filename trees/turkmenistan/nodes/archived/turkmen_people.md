# Turkmen People

## Summary
The Turkmen people are a Turkic ethnic group associated with Turkmenistan and surrounding regions of Central Asia, Iran, and Afghanistan.

## Notes
Key branches include origins, Oghuz Turk connections, tribal organization, migration patterns, language, and settlement around oases and steppe zones.
